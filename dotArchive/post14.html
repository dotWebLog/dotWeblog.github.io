The Forgotten Draft

I stared at the unsent message. Words I had arranged carefully, pressed together in a moment of bravery.

Between “send” and “delete,” the silence grew heavy. The cursor blinked like a quiet question: Will you speak, or stay hidden?

I closed the tab. The draft stayed behind, trapped in the inbox of almost-confessions, where words live without wings.
