The Notification Bubble

One red circle appeared. Small, almost nothing. Yet it pulled my attention like gravity itself.

Inside that bubble could be anything—an invitation, a rejection, a reminder I mattered, or proof I’d been forgotten.

I tapped. The moment burst. The mystery was gone, leaving behind only the usual emptiness of the feed.
