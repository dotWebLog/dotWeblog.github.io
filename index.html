> Hello there!

After a lot of thought and behind-the-scenes planning, I’m excited to share that this blog is moving to a new home!

Blogger has been a great starting point for this journey, and I’m so thankful for everyone who has followed along here. But as this blog has grown, I’ve felt the need for a space that offers more flexibility, better design, and a few features that can support the next phase of what I want to share.

So… I’m moving to dotweblog.blogspot.com

Don’t worry—this isn’t goodbye, just a change of address. All new posts, updates, and exciting content will be published over there. I’ll also be updating the look and feel of the blog to make it more reader-friendly and engaging.

If you’ve enjoyed reading here, I’d love for you to join me at the new site.

Enjoy your journey!
