> Blogging Podcasts

There are several podcasts that are highly regarded for providing valuable insights and tips about blogging. However, the podcast landscape evolves quickly, and new shows may have emerged.

Here are some podcasts that were well-regarded for blogging and content creation:

  1. ProBlogger Podcast: The ProBlogger Podcast covers a wide range of topics related to blogging, including content creation, monetisation strategies, and building an audience.
    • Host: Darren Rowse
    • Focus: The ProBlogger Podcast is dedicated to helping bloggers improve their craft. Darren Rowse shares insights on blog monetisation, content creation, building traffic, and staying motivated. The podcast often features interviews with successful bloggers.
  2. The Smart Passive Income Podcast: This podcast delves into various online business strategies, including blogging. It covers topics like passive income, entrepreneurship, and online marketing.
    • Host: Pat Flynn
    • Focus: Pat Flynn's podcast covers a wide range of topics related to online business and passive income. While not exclusively focused on blogging, it includes valuable insights into building successful online platforms, creating compelling content, and various monetisation strategies.
  3. Blogging Your Passion Podcast: This podcast focuses on helping individuals turn their passion into a profitable blog. It covers blogging strategies, content creation, and building an online business around your passions.
    • Host: Jonathan Milligan
    • Focus: This podcast is centered around helping individuals turn their passions into profitable blogs. Topics include finding your niche, effective content creation, and strategies for building a business around your blog.
  4. The Blogging Millionaire: This podcast shares insights and strategies on building a successful blog. The podcast covers various aspects of blogging, from SEO and content creation to monetisation.
    • Host: Brandon Gaille
    • Focus: Brandon Gaille shares practical tips and strategies for building a successful blog. The podcast covers topics such as search engine optimisation (SEO), content marketing, and various methods for monetising a blog.
  5. Copyblogger FM: Copyblogger FM is the podcast of Copyblogger, a well-known resource for content marketers. The podcast covers copywriting, content marketing, and blogging strategies.
    • Host: Sonia Simone
    • Focus: Copyblogger FM, associated with the Copyblogger platform, delves into the art of copywriting, content marketing, and building an effective online presence. It provides insights into crafting compelling content and growing an audience.
  6. Blogger Genius Podcast: This podcast focuses on interviewing successful bloggers and online entrepreneurs, providing actionable tips and insights for growing a blog.
    • Host: Jillian Leslie
    • Focus: Jillian Leslie interviews successful bloggers and online entrepreneurs to extract insights and actionable tips for growing a blog. The podcast covers a wide range of topics, including social media strategies, content creation, and business growth.
  7. The Side Hustle School: While not exclusively about blogging, this podcast features daily episodes highlighting different side hustle success stories, including many related to blogging and online businesses.
    • Host: Chris Guillebeau
    • Focus: While not exclusively focused on blogging, The Side Hustle School features daily episodes highlighting real-life stories of people who have turned their side hustles into successful ventures. It provides inspiration and practical insights into building online businesses.
  8. The Influencer Podcast: The Influencer Podcast covers various aspects of influencer marketing, which can be relevant for bloggers looking to grow their influence and reach.
    • Host: Julie Solomon
    • Focus: The Influencer Podcast explores influencer marketing strategies, which can be beneficial for bloggers looking to expand their reach and influence. It covers topics like building a personal brand, collaboration, and effective communication.

If you're looking to enhance your blogging skills and stay informed about the latest trends in content creation, monetisation strategies, and online business, consider exploring the podcasts mentioned above.

Check for the latest episodes and reviews to ensure that the content aligns with your current blogging goals and interests. Podcasts can be valuable resources for staying informed, gaining inspiration, and learning from the experiences of successful bloggers and online entrepreneurs.
